CHARACTERED YOUTH

At this moment ...

I am standing...

Trying to understand

Where do I set my foot on.

Where I string words

Where I aspire to

How grateful I am

My strapping for Indonesia

Look at my work

I will carve my achievements

Enthusiastic with a steel mentality

Extraordinary spirit for the nation

Haiii young friend ..

Let's color our lives

With millions of aspirational inspirations

The goal for the sake of success

Remember our nation is always waiting.

We will welcome the era of globalization.

The presence of creative works full of sensation.

For the sake of this beloved country

homeland of Indonesia, motherland.
