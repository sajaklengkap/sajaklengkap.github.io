Merokok

Merokok merupakan kebiasaan buruk yang harus kita hindari. Banyak orang mulai merokok karena tekanan teman sebaya, depresi, kebutuhan untuk menurunkan berat badan, atau untuk bersantai. In a group discussion of 3 or 4 people, the following questions can be discussed:

1. Who are the characters?

- Identify the main characters in the story and discuss their roles, characteristics, and relationships with each other.

2. When did the story take place?

- Determine the time period or era in which the events of the story occurred. Consider any specific historical or cultural references that may provide clues.

3. Where did the story take place?

- Identify the setting or locations where the story unfolds. Discuss the significance of the setting and how it influences the events and characters.

4. What happened to the characters?

- Summarize the main events or plot points that occurred in the story. Discuss the challenges, conflicts, or changes that the characters experienced.

5. What challenges did the characters encounter in the story?

- Analyze the obstacles or difficulties faced by the characters. Discuss how these challenges affected their actions, decisions, and relationships.

6. How did the characters solve the problem?

- Explore the strategies, actions, or decisions taken by the characters to overcome the challenges they faced. Discuss the effectiveness or consequences of their solutions.

7. How was the ending of the story?

- Discuss the outcome or resolution of the story. Was it a happy or sad ending? Were all conflicts resolved? Analyze the impact of the ending on the characters and the overall message of the story.
