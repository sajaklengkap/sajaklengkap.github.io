We went to high school together, where we had good times, and today I want to give a big congratulations to everyone including myself for being here today. The world we live in is plagued with dangerous: Ebola, ISIS, Global Warming, facial acne, COVID-19, and despite all the odds, we still managed to graduate, so let’s give us a thunder of claps and round of applause.

It was only 3 years ago that we came to this academy known as…………… as young, timid sophomores we found ourselves lost in its halls like there were the changing staircases of Hogwarts.

Though nervous as sophomores, we started getting a hang of things, and Junior year for many of us brought experience and for many more of us, it meant Stage 1 Senioritis. And now, here we are: done with High School!

Jawaban:

English

FAREWELL SPEECH

With all respect, good morning rector, dean, lecturers, senior, and my friends. Thank you God for Your grace that today we can gather today. This is truly an honor to stand at this podium, delivering farewell speech in our graduation day.

Dear my friends that I’m proud of,

This is the day. When we wear our graduation uniform, coming together in this ballroom. There are thousand days that we went together through ups and downs. My mind still wandered to the first time we came to this college, as new students. At that time, we were so proud, being the chosen from the best. The first day we saw new faces, full of spirit. We were welcomed with open hands by the college where we have been studying until now. We had great spirit to start the day as University student.

Until now, hunders days later, the spirit is still lingers. The difference is, we are now feel content of what we have achieved. We succeed being part of our beloved University. There were many days full of stories. We studied the lessons, also the lecturers with their own characters. We tried to understand the learning system in this University. We tried new non-academic experiences that suited our passion.

One thing that I always hold on to during my journey in this University is that I should be a curious soul to new things. In positive matter, of course. College is the place to process and becoming new person, ready to mingle to the real life. College is the place to lead ourself. We are the one who is in charge of our responsibility as University student.

Dear my happy friends,

When I was a University student, I felt the ups and downs in life. Got low grade, made the lecturer mad, and once I had no idea what to do. This college is the real witness of what happened to someone during his self-actualization phase. I got new friends who are now become family. I looked up to seniors, learning from their experience. I also got many advices from the lecturers, not only about lessons but also about life.

Our success as University student is not determined by our GPA. It is not measured by how many trophy and certificate that we got. Also not defined by our workplace later. The most important thing is the process during our learning journey. The process with all the stories in it is the real life path that we will keep in our heart forever.

Dear friends that I’m proud of,

Starting from tomorrow we have new status in our shoulder. We will spread our wings, leaving this college. We will enter the real life. What we are now is different with the moment when we came here the first time. This journey will be our norm. No word can express how proud and gratitude I am for being part of this University. Thank you everyone that I know for being part of my long journey during my study.

Bill Gates once said in his speech, be a hungry soul. Hungry means we become a person that keeps on looking for new experiences. Let’s be a person that gives benefit to the society, be a person that our University will be proud of. Let me say my sincere apologize if I did something wrong to you.

Goodbye, everyone. This is not the end. I hope our path will cross someday in great endeavour.

Cheers.
